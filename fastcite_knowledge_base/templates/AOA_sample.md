# ARTICLES OF ASSOCIATION

## PRIVATE LIMITED COMPANY / SINGLE MEMBER COMPANY

**This is a SAMPLE template. Always consult a lawyer for your specific case.**

---

## HEADING

THE ARTICLES OF ASSOCIATION

of

**[COMPANY NAME] (PVT.) LIMITED**

---

## PART 1: MEMBERSHIP & SHARE CAPITAL

### Article 1: Members & Shares

1.1 Every person whose name is entered in the register of members is a member of the Company.

1.2 No person shall be admitted as a member of the Company unless they have agreed to take at least one share and have paid the full subscription price.

1.3 The register of members shall be kept at the registered office and shall be open to inspection.

### Article 2: Transfer of Shares

2.1 Shares may be transferred to any person, but the Company may refuse to register the transfer if:
   - The transferor has not paid all they owe to the Company
   - The transfer is not in writing and signed by or on behalf of the transferor

2.2 All transfer documents must be produced to the Company before registration.

2.3 The registration of transfers cannot be refused without just cause.

---

## PART 2: DIRECTORS

### Article 3: Directors' Powers

3.1 The management of the Company shall be vested in the Directors, who may exercise all such powers of the Company as are not required by law or these Articles to be exercised in general meeting.

3.2 Directors shall act honestly, faithfully, and in the best interests of the Company.

3.3 Directors shall not enter into contracts with the Company or any other arrangement in which they have a financial interest without prior disclosure and board approval.

### Article 4: Number & Appointment of Directors

4.1 The number of Directors shall be:
   - **For SMC:** Minimum 1 Director (the sole member)
   - **For Pvt Ltd:** Minimum 2 Directors

4.2 The first Directors are named in the Memorandum of Association.

4.3 Directors shall be appointed by ordinary resolution of the members in general meeting.

4.4 Every Director shall be a natural person of at least 18 years of age and a resident of Pakistan, unless exempted by SECP.

### Article 5: Disqualifications

5.1 A person shall be disqualified from being a Director if they:
   - Are an undischarged bankrupt
   - Have been convicted of a crime involving dishonesty
   - Have been disqualified under the Companies Act 2017
   - Are of unsound mind

### Article 6: Retirement & Removal

6.1 Directors hold office for such period as the members determine.

6.2 Directors may retire by written notice to the Company.

6.3 Directors may be removed by ordinary resolution before expiry of their term.

6.4 Removal does not prejudice claims for damages for breach of contract.

### Article 7: Directors' Remuneration

7.1 Directors' remuneration shall be such as the members determine by ordinary resolution.

7.2 A Director may be paid for services over and above their remuneration as set by resolution.

7.3 Directors shall not receive commission, bonus, or percentage of profits without approval.

---

## PART 3: BOARD MEETINGS

### Article 8: Board Meetings

8.1 The Directors may meet together for the despatch of business, adjourn, and otherwise regulate their meetings.

8.2 A Director may call a board meeting by giving at least 7 days' written notice (or shorter if urgent).

8.3 Board meetings may be held in person or via video conference if agreed.

8.4 The quorum for a board meeting shall be the number of Directors present and constituting a majority.

### Article 9: Minutes

9.1 The Directors shall keep minutes of all meetings and resolutions.

9.2 Minutes shall be kept at the registered office and be available for inspection.

9.3 Minutes signed by the chair are evidence of the proceedings.

---

## PART 4: FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT

### Article 10: Financial Year

10.1 The financial year of the Company shall be from [1 JULY] to [30 JUNE], or as determined by the Directors.

10.2 The financial statements shall be prepared annually in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) or applicable accounting standards.

### Article 11: Dividends

11.1 Dividends shall only be paid out of profits available for distribution.

11.2 Dividends shall be declared and paid as determined by the Directors or by member resolution.

11.3 Dividends shall be apportioned equally among shares of the same class.

### Article 12: Accounts & Audit

12.1 The Directors shall maintain proper accounting records.

12.2 Annual financial statements shall be audited by an external auditor (if required by law).

12.3 Audited accounts shall be presented to members at the annual general meeting.

12.4 Accounts shall be filed with SECP within 120 days of financial year-end.

---

## PART 5: GENERAL MEETINGS

### Article 13: Annual General Meeting (AGM)

13.1 An Annual General Meeting shall be held once in every calendar year, within 120 days of financial year-end.

13.2 Business transacted at AGM shall include:
   - Presentation of accounts
   - Appointment of auditor
   - Declaration of dividends
   - Election of Directors (if required)

### Article 14: Notice of Meetings

14.1 Not less than 21 days' written notice shall be given to members before any general meeting.

14.2 Notice shall specify the venue, time, date, and agenda.

14.3 Notice may be given by hand, email, or courier.

### Article 15: Quorum

15.1 Quorum for a general meeting shall be:
   - **For SMC:** The sole member
   - **For Pvt Ltd:** Two members present (in person or by proxy)

15.2 Lack of quorum shall not invalidate any proceedings if all members entitled to vote are present or represented.

### Article 16: Voting

16.1 Every member has one vote per share held.

16.2 Voting shall be by show of hands or, if demanded, by ballot.

16.3 A proxy may vote on behalf of a member if authorized in writing.

16.4 Ordinary resolutions require votes from a simple majority of members.

16.5 Special resolutions require votes from at least 75% of members.

---

## PART 6: WINDING UP

### Article 17: Dissolution

17.1 The Company may be wound up by the SECP at the request of members.

17.2 Assets after payment of liabilities shall be distributed among members in proportion to their shareholding.

## IMPORTANT NOTES FOR USER:

1. **Use approved sample:** Get SECP standard Articles template from [eservices.secp.gov.pk](https://eservices.secp.gov.pk)
2. **Customize:** Only modify sections relevant to your company (keep rest standard)
3. **Director details:** Replace with actual director names, CNICs
4. **Financial year:** Choose dates that work for your audit cycle (many use July-June)
5. **All articles:** Must be signed and submitted with Memorandum
6. **Legal review:** Have a lawyer review before submission to SECP
