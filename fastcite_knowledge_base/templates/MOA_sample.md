## 3. OBJECTS

The objects for which the Company is established are:

### Primary Objects:
1. To engage in **[PRIMARY BUSINESS ACTIVITY - e.g., "software development and IT consulting"]**

2. To provide services related to **[SECONDARY BUSINESS - e.g., "web design, digital marketing"]**

3. To establish, promote, develop, conduct and maintain commercial and professional activities.

### Secondary Objects:
1. To acquire, hold, sell, lease, transfer, and deal in any description of property, both real and personal.

2. To borrow and raise money and to issue debentures and other securities.

3. To invest the funds of the Company in such stocks, bonds, securities, or other investments as the Directors may think fit.

4. To enter into contracts for the provision of services similar to those described above.

## IMPORTANT NOTES FOR USER:

1. **Obtain SECP template:** Go to [eservices.secp.gov.pk](https://eservices.secp.gov.pk) and download the latest MoA template - use that as your base
2. **Professional help:** Use a lawyer or SECP consultant to fill this in correctly
3. **Company name:** Must match exactly as approved by SECP
4. **Capital amount:** Should be realistic for your business size
5. **Objects section:** Try to be specific but broad enough for future expansion
6. **All signatures:** Must be done digitally or with notarization
7. **Keep copies:** Save 10+ printed and PDF copies after approval
