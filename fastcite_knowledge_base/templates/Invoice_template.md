## HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE

### Step 1: Customize Header
- [ ] Add your company name
- [ ] Add your NTN (required for tax compliance)
- [ ] Add STRN if you're registered for sales tax
- [ ] Add office address and contact details

### Step 2: Fill Invoice Details
- [ ] Give invoice a unique number (e.g., INV-2026-001, INV-2026-002)
- [ ] Invoice date = date you're sending it
- [ ] Due date = typically 30 days later
- [ ] Client name and CNIC/NTN

### Step 3: Describe What You Did
- [ ] Be specific (e.g., "Website design for 3 weeks" not "Services")
- [ ] List deliverables/hours/scope
- [ ] This becomes your proof of income for tax purposes

### Step 4: Set Amount & Taxes
- [ ] Subtotal = amount before tax
- [ ] If you collect sales tax: add 17% (if STRN registered)
- [ ] Most sole proprietors/freelancers: no sales tax (mark 0%)
- [ ] Total = what client must pay you

### Step 5: Give Payment Details
- [ ] Bank account or mobile payment option
- [ ] Make it easy for client to pay

### Step 6: Set Due Date
- [ ] Typically 30 days
- [ ] Mention late payment interest (optional: 1.5% per month)

### Step 7: Sign & Send
- [ ] Add your signature (or digital signature)
- [ ] Send soft copy via email
- [ ] Keep copy for your records

## IMPORTANT FOR TAX COMPLIANCE

### Tax-Related Rules:

1. **NTN Requirement:**
   - Display your NTN on every invoice (REQUIRED)
   - Missing NTN = risk of audit by FBR

2. **Sales Tax (if STRN registered):**
   - If you have STRN, you MUST charge 17% sales tax
   - Invoice must show tax separately

3. **Record Keeping:**
   - Keep every invoice issued (5-year requirement)
   - Tax authority may ask for copies
   - Digital or printed copies acceptable

4. **Income Declaration:**
   - All invoiced amounts = your income
   - Must declare on annual tax return by Sept 30
   - Sum of invoices ≠ profit (minus expenses)

5. **Client Withholding Tax:**
   - Some clients will withhold 3-5% tax from payment
   - This is normal (don't panic - credited to you)
   - Show on your tax return

## DO'S & DON'Ts

| DO | DON'T |
|---|---|
| ✅ Issue invoice for every job | ❌ Accept cash without receipt |
| ✅ Number invoices sequentially | ❌ Forget to mention NTN |
| ✅ Keep invoices organized | ❌ Invoice for fake work |
| ✅ Save copies digitally | ❌ Over-state hours/work done |
| ✅ Be specific about work description | ❌ Invoice with obvious spelling errors |
| ✅ Show tax separately | ❌ Hide income from clients |
