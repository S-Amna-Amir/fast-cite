# PARTNERSHIP DEED

**This is a SAMPLE template for a 2-3 person partnership. Always consult a lawyer for your specific case.**

---

## HEADING

**PARTNERSHIP DEED**

Made this [DATE] day of [MONTH], 2026

**BETWEEN**

1. **[PARTNER 1 FULL NAME]**, son of [FATHER'S NAME], aged [AGE], CNIC # [CNIC], resident of [ADDRESS] (hereinafter called "Partner 1")

2. **[PARTNER 2 FULL NAME]**, son of [FATHER'S NAME], aged [AGE], CNIC # [CNIC], resident of [ADDRESS] (hereinafter called "Partner 2")

3. **[PARTNER 3 FULL NAME]**, son of [FATHER'S NAME], aged [AGE], CNIC # [CNIC], resident of [ADDRESS] (hereinafter called "Partner 3") [if applicable]

(Hereinafter collectively called the "Partners" and individually called the "Partner")

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS the Partners wish to form a partnership firm for the purpose of carrying on business of [BUSINESS DESCRIPTION - e.g., "software development", "consulting", "trading"], and for that purpose they have agreed to enter into this partnership.

NOW IT IS HEREBY AGREED as follows:

---

## 1. NAME OF FIRM

The firm shall be registered under the name and style of **[FIRM NAME]** at [OFFICE ADDRESS].

---

## 2. PARTNERS & SHARES

The partners and their shares in the profits and losses of the firm are:

| Partner Name | Share (%) | Capital Contribution (PKR) |
|---|---|---|
| [Partner 1] | 50% | [Amount] |
| [Partner 2] | 30% | [Amount] |
| [Partner 3] | 20% | [Amount] |
| **Total** | **100%** | **[Total Amount]** |

---

## 3. CAPITAL OF THE FIRM

3.1 Each partner shall bring in capital as mentioned above within 30 days of this deed.

3.2 Capital brought in shall remain the property of the partner who brought it.

3.3 No partner shall draw their capital without consent of all other partners.

3.4 Additional capital required for the firm's operations shall be contributed proportionally unless agreed otherwise.

---

## 4. PLACE OF BUSINESS

The principal place of business of the partnership shall be at:

**[COMPLETE OFFICE ADDRESS]**
[City], [Province], [Postal Code]

The partnership may open branch offices with the consent of all partners.

---

## 5. NATURE OF BUSINESS

The firm shall business of:
- [PRIMARY BUSINESS ACTIVITY - e.g., "software development and IT solutions"]
- [SECONDARY ACTIVITY - e.g., "web design and digital marketing services"]
- [OTHER ACTIVITIES - e.g., "consultancy and advisory services"]

---

## 6. DURATION

6.1 This partnership shall commence from **[START DATE]** and shall continue until dissolved in accordance with provisions of this deed.

6.2 The partnership may be dissolved at any time:
   - By mutual written consent of all partners
   - On death of a partner
   - On retirement of a partner (with notice)
   - On insolvency of a partner
   - On breach of this agreement

---

## 7. PROFIT & LOSS

7.1 All profits of the firm shall be divided among partners in the proportions mentioned in Clause 2.

7.2 All losses of the firm shall be borne by partners in the same proportions.

7.3 No partner shall be entitled to salary, commission, or interest on capital except as provided in Clause 8.

---

## 8. DRAWINGS & REMUNERATION

8.1 Each partner may draw [AMOUNT] per month as salary/drawing. (To be specified - e.g., "PKR 50,000 per month")

8.2 Drawings shall be adjusted against each partner's share of profit at year-end.

8.3 Additional remuneration may be paid to a partner for special services with consent of other partners.

---

## 9. ACCOUNTS & FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT

9.1 The firm's financial year shall be [DATES - e.g., "from 1 July to 30 June"].

9.2 Proper books of accounts shall be maintained at the principal office.

9.3 Accounts shall be balanced and finalized on [DATE] every year.

9.4 Annual accounts shall be prepared, audited (if required), and shared with all partners.

9.5 Each partner has the right to inspect account books at reasonable notice.

---

## 10. BANK ACCOUNTS

10.1 The firm shall maintain a bank account in the firm's name.

10.2 Bank account signatories:
   - [PARTNER 1 NAME]
   - [PARTNER 2 NAME]
   - [Any/All signatures required for cheques above PKR XXXXX]

10.3 All cheques above PKR [AMOUNT] shall require signatures of at least [2/ALL] partners.

---

## 11. MANAGEMENT & DECISIONS

11.1 All partners shall have equal rights in the management of the firm unless agreed otherwise.

11.2 Routine decisions (daily operations) may be made by any one partner.

11.3 Major decisions require consent of all partners:
   - Borrowing money exceeding PKR [AMOUNT]
   - Acquisition of real property
   - Admission of new partner
   - Change of nature of business
   - Dissolution of firm

11.4 Disputes between partners shall be resolved by:
   - First: Discussion between partners
   - Second: Mediation (if amicable solution wanted)
   - Third: Legal proceeding (if necessary)

---

## 12. ADMISSION OF NEW PARTNERS

12.1 No new partner shall be admitted to the firm without the written consent of all existing partners.

12.2 An incoming partner shall contribute capital as agreed and shall sign a fresh deed or supplementary deed.

---

## 13. RETIREMENT OF A PARTNER

13.1 A partner wishing to retire must give [3/6/12] months' written notice to all other partners.

13.2 Upon retirement:
   - Share of goodwill (if any) shall be determined by mutual agreement
   - Share of capital and profit due shall be paid within [30/60] days
   - Remaining partners may continue or dissolve

13.3 A retiring partner shall not carry on similar business within [RADIUS - e.g., "50 km"] for [TIME PERIOD - e.g., "2 years"].

---

## 14. DEATH OF A PARTNER

14.1 On death of a partner:
   - Firm shall be dissolved unless agreement otherwise
   - Deceased partner's share shall pass to their legal heirs
   - Remaining partners may decide to continue with heirs OR wind up the firm

14.2 Settlement of dues to deceased partner's estate:
   - Share of capital shall be paid
   - Share of profit up to date of death
   - Share of goodwill (if applicable)
   - All payments within [60/90] days

---

## 15. DISPUTES & ARBITRATION

15.1 All disputes related to this partnership shall be resolved by arbitration.

15.2 Each partner shall appoint one arbitrator, and the two arbitrators shall appoint a third.

15.3 The arbitrator's award shall be final and binding on all partners.

---

## 16. EXPULSION

16.1 A partner may be expelled if:
   - They commit fraud or dishonesty
   - They breach this deed materially
   - They are declared bankrupt
   - They are convicted of a crime

16.2 Expulsion requires written notice and opportunity to respond.

16.3 Expelled partner shall receive their capital and goodwill share.

---

## 17. DISSOLUTION

17.1 Upon dissolution:
   - All debts shall be paid from firm assets
   - Remaining assets divided according to profit-sharing ratio
   - All books and records kept for at least 5 years
   - Firm registration cancelled with Registrar

---

## 18. CONFIDENTIALITY

18.1 Partners shall maintain confidentiality of firm's trade secrets, client lists, and financial information.

18.2 This obligation continues after a partner leaves the firm.

---

## 19. NON-COMPETENCE

19.1 No partner shall engage in competing business while in partnership without consent of all partners.

19.2 On leaving, a partner agrees not to carry competing business within [TIME/LOCATION] as specified in Clause 13.

---

## 20. MISCELLANEOUS

20.1 This deed shall be governed by the laws of Pakistan (Partnership Act 1932).

20.2 Any amendment must be in writing and signed by all partners.

20.3 This deed constitutes the entire agreement; no oral modifications are valid.

## IMPORTANT NOTES FOR USER:

1. **Get it notarized:** Have all signatures witnessed by a Notary Public (adds legal weight)
2. **Customize clauses:** Adjust capital amounts, profit-sharing, remuneration per your agreement
3. **Clear exit rules:** Especially important - specify what happens when partner leaves
4. **Bank signatory:** Specify who can sign cheques (reduces fraud)
5. **Keep copies:** Distribution to all partners and Registrar office
6. **Register with Registrar of Firms** within 30 days of execution
7. **Professional review:** Have lawyer review before signing with partners
8. **Multiple copies:** Print 5+ originals (one per partner + registrar + bank + records)
