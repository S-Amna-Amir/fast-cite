# Registration Timeline: What to Expect

Plan realistically. This guide shows actual timelines and what causes delays.

---

## SOLE PROPRIETORSHIP: NTN Registration

### Timeline: 1-5 Days (Fastest)

**What You'll Do:**
1. Go to [iris.fbr.gov.pk](https://iris.fbr.gov.pk)
2. Click "Registration for Unregistered Person"
3. Enter CNIC, name, mobile, email
4. Verify OTPs (5 min)
5. Submit Form 181
6. FBR processes
7. Check status after 1-5 days

**Realistic Breakdown:**
- Registration: **15 minutes**
- OTP verification: **10 minutes**
- FBR processing: **1-5 days** (depends on their workload)
- **Total: 1-5 business days**

### Common Blockers & Delays

| Blocker | Problem | Solution | Delay |
|---------|---------|----------|-------|
| Wrong CNIC | Name mismatch | Recheck CNIC spelling | 1-2 days |
| Inactive mobile | OTP won't arrive | Use current mobile number | 1 day |
| Email typo | Can't verify email OTP | Fill correct email | 1 day |
| Server issues | IRIS portal down | Try next day or wait | 1-2 days |
| FBR backlog | High volume period | Submit early in month | 2-5 days |

### Fast-Track Option
**None.** NTN registration is already the fastest. No express option.

### Cost: PKR 0 (Free)

## SMC: SECP Registration

### Timeline: 1-2 Weeks (With Consultant) OR 2-3 Days (Documents Ready)

**Best Case (All documents prepared):**
| Step | Time | Who Does It |
|------|------|---------|
| Name search on SECP | 1 hour | You on eServices |
| Get digital subscriber certificate | 1-2 days | Third party provider |
| Draft MoA/AoA using templates | 1-2 days | You (or consultant) |
| Prepare other docs (address proof, etc) | 1 day | You |
| Submit Form 1 to SECP | 15 min | You on eServices |
| SECP processing & approval | 2-3 days | SECP team |
| Download certificate | 5 min | You from eServices |
| **Total: 1 week** | | |

**Realistic Case (With consultant help):**
| Step | Time |
|------|------|
| Contact consultant | 1 day |
| Consultant prepares documents | 3-5 days |
| You review & sign | 1-2 days |
| Consultant submits | 1 day |
| SECP processing | 2-3 days |
| **Total: 1-2 weeks** | |

### Common Blockers & Delays

| Blocker | Problem | Impact | Delay |
|---------|---------|--------|-------|
| **Name Unavailable** | Chosen name already exists | Must search again/choose new name | 2-5 days |
| **Address Proof Issues** | Utility bill name doesn't match CNIC | Get utility in your name | 5-10 days |
| **Digital Signature Problem** | Invalid or expired | Reapply at certificate provider | 2-3 days |
| **MoA/AoA Errors** | Grammatical or legal issues | Revise and resubmit | 3 days |
| **Document Not Verified** | SECP can't verify address | Resubmit with attested copies | 3-5 days |
| **SECP Backlog** | High volume period | Submit and wait | 5-10 days |
| **You Miss Email OTP** | Verification email expires after 24h | Resubmit form | 1 day |

### Worst Case Scenario
**Name unavailable → Must search again → Get new documents → Resubmit = 3-4 weeks total**

### Fast-Track Option
**No official express lane**, but hiring a reputable consultant (2-3 day delivery) can reduce delays from document preparation.

## PRIVATE LIMITED COMPANY: SECP Registration

### Timeline: 1-2 Weeks (With Consultant) OR 2-3 Days (Documents Ready)

**Realistic Process:**
| Step | Time | Complexity |
|------|------|---------|
| Founders agreement (cap table, roles, etc) | 1-3 days | High - must decide structure |
| Name search (3 options) | 1 hour | Low |
| Get digital signatures (2+ people) | 2-3 days | Medium - coordinate with founders |
| Draft MoA/AoA (more complex than SMC) | 3-5 days | High - more governance provisions |
| Prepare director/shareholder docs | 1-2 days | Low |
| Get address proof | 1-2 days | Low-Medium |
| Submit Form 1 | 15 min | Low |
| SECP processing | 2-3 days | Depends on SECP |
| **Total: 1-2 weeks** | |

### Common Blockers - More Complex Than SMC

| Blocker | Problem | Impact | Delay |
|---------|---------|--------|-------|
| **Founders Can't Agree** | Equity %, roles unclear | Can't draft documents | 3-5 days+ |
| **Name Not Available** | Pvt Ltd names get taken fast | Must brainstorm new names | 2 days |
| **Address Proof Issues** | Multiple founders, one office | Office must be in ONE founder's name initially | 5 days |
| **Too Many Signatories** | All directors must sign digitally | Coordinating 3-4 people's schedules | 3-5 days |
| **Digital Signature Validity** | Signatures not matching spec | Redo at certificate authority | 2-3 days |
| **MoA/AoA Complexity** | Missing governance clauses | SECP may request amendments | 3-5 days |
| **Shareholder Verification** | SECP verifies all shareholders | Follow up required | 2 days |
| **SECP Holds for Review** | High-value companies checked more | Deliberate delay by SECP | 5-10 days |

### Worst Case
**Founders disagree on equity → redraft → name taken → new address proof → digital signature issues → SECP queries = 4-6 weeks**

### Reality Check: Investor Due Diligence Delay
Even after certificate received, investors often require:
- Certified board resolutions (2 days)
- Share certificate originals (1 day)
- Cap table verification (1-2 days)
- Compliance certificate from lawyer (2-3 days)
- **= Additional 1 week before investment closes**

## PARTNERSHIP: Registrar of Firms

### Timeline: 2-3 Weeks

**Process:**
| Step | Time |
|------|------|
| Draft partnership deed with partners | 3-5 days |
| Get deed notarized (optional but recommended) | 1 day |
| Publish in newspaper (required) | 1 day + 2 days for publication cycle |
| Prepare registration application | 1 day |
| Submit to provincial Registrar of Firms | 1 day |
| Registrar review & approval | 7-10 days |
| Get registration certificate | 1 day |
| **Total: 2-3 weeks** |

### Common Blockers

| Blocker | Problem | Impact | Delay |
|---------|---------|--------|-------|
| **Partners Can't Agree** | Profit-sharing, roles unclear | Deed writing stalls | 5-7 days |
| **Newspaper Notice Timing** | Chose wrong publication date | Must wait next cycle | 3-5 days |
| **Address Disagreement** | Registrar office location varies | May need to register provincially | 1 day |
| **Registrar Backlog** | High volume period | Waiting for approval | 10-20 days |
| **Missing Signatures** | One partner unavailable | Deed rejected, restart | 3 days |

### Provincial Variations
- **Punjab (Lahore/Islamabad):** 7-10 days (faster, more volume)
- **Sindh (Karachi):** 10-14 days
- **KPK (Peshawar):** 7-10 days
- **Balochistan:** 2-3 weeks (slower)

## HOW TO SPEED UP (Realistic Tips)

### For SMC/Pvt Ltd:
1. **Prepare docs yourself first** (use SECP templates) → Save 3-5 days of consultant prep
2. **Get digital signature ready ASAP** (process takes 1-2 days) → Don't wait until filing
3. **Have address proof ready** (utility bill in your name) → Avoid delays
4. **Triple-check form before submitting** → Avoid resubmission delays
5. **Choose a name that's unique** → Avoid name search rejection

### For NTN:
- File early in the month (less FBR backlog)
- Use current mobile number
- Double-check CNIC spelling
- Can't speed up beyond 5 days

### For Partnership:
- Draft deed early with all partners
- Publish in newspaper on Monday (ready by Wednesday)
- Register immediately after publication
- Pick registrar office near you

## BUFFER PLANNING FOR FOUNDERS

- **Testing idea (NTN):** 1 week buffer (actual: 3-5 days)
- **Solo professional (SMC):** 3-4 weeks buffer (actual: 1-2 weeks) = margin for issues
- **Seeking funding (Pvt Ltd):** 4-6 weeks buffer (actual: 1-2 weeks) = catch blocks early
- **Partnership:** 4 weeks buffer (actual: 2-3 weeks)

**Pro Tip:** Start registration process 4-6 weeks before you actually need the company operational. Covers delays.
