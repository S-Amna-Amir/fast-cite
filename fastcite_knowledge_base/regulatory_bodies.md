# Who Registers What: Pakistan's Authorities

Confused about which government body does what? This explains Pakistan's legal authorities.

---

## SECP - The Company Authority

**Full Name:** Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan

**What they do:**
- Register companies (Private Limited, SMCs)
- Oversee corporate governance
- Regulate capital markets
- Enforce company law

### Which businesses register with SECP?
- ✅ Private Limited Companies
- ✅ Single Member Companies (SMC)
- ❌ NOT sole proprietorships
- ❌ NOT partnerships (partnerships go to Registrar of Firms)
- ❌ NOT freelancers (they just get NTN from FBR)

### What you get from SECP:
- Certificate of Incorporation (proves company exists)
- Company registration number
- Approval for company name

### When you interact with SECP:
1. **Registration:** Initial company registration
2. **Annual Returns:** File accounts every year
3. **Changes:** Notify if directors/shareholders change
4. **Compliance:** Annual fees and filings

### SECP vs FBR - Key Difference
| SECP | FBR |
|------|-----|
| **Registers:** Companies only | **Registers:** ALL business structures + individuals |
| **Website:** [eservices.secp.gov.pk](https://eservices.secp.gov.pk) | **Website:** [iris.fbr.gov.pk](https://iris.fbr.gov.pk) |
| **What they track:** Ownership, governance | **What they track:** Income, taxes |
| **Certificate:** Certificate of Incorporation | **Certificate:** NTN (tax number) |
| **Covers:** Corporate structure | **Covers:** Tax obligations |

## FBR - The Tax Authority

**Full Name:** Federal Board of Revenue

**What they do:**
- Register all tax numbers (NTN)
- Collect income tax
- Manage sales tax (STRN)
- Audit tax filers
- Enforce tax compliance

### Who registers with FBR?
- ✅ Individuals (for NTN)
- ✅ All businesses (regardless of structure)
- ✅ Sole proprietors (REQUIRED)
- ✅ Freelancers (REQUIRED for bank account)
- ✅ Company directors (personal NTN + company NTN)

### What you get from FBR:
- **NTN:** National Tax Number (required for bank account)
- **Filer Status:** Designation after filing first return
- **STRN:** Sales Tax Registration Number (if applicable)
- **Tax Returns:** Filed online annually

### When you interact with FBR:
1. **Registration:** Get NTN via [iris.fbr.gov.pk](https://iris.fbr.gov.pk)
2. **Annual Filing:** File income tax return by Sept 30
3. **Sales Tax:** File monthly returns if STRN registered
4. **Audits:** May be audited if selected

---

## REGISTRAR OF FIRMS - The Partnership Authority

**What they do:**
- Register partnership firms (NOT companies)
- Maintain partnership registry
- Provide partnership certificates

### Who registers here?
- ✅ Partnership firms (2-20 partners)
- ❌ NOT sole proprietors
- ❌ NOT companies

### What you get:
- Partnership registration certificate
- Partnership registry entry
- Legal recognition

### Unique feature:
**PROVINCIAL:** Each province has its own Registrar of Firms
- Punjab: Lahore registrar
- Sindh: Karachi registrar
- KPK: Peshawar registrar
- Balochistan: Quetta registrar

---

## SBP - The Banking Authority

**Full Name:** State Bank of Pakistan

**What they do:**
- Regulate banks
- Set monetary policy
- Monitor financial institutions
- Set banking regulations

### What you need from SBP:
- ❌ Nothing directly (you don't register with SBP)
- ✅ Banks follow SBP rules (so accounts are safe)

## DECISION TREE: Which Authority Registers Me?

```
Start: What's your business structure?

├─ I'm a solo person running a business
│  └─ Only NTN from FBR (iris.fbr.gov.pk)
│     └─ Cost: PKR 0
│
├─ I want a company with 1 person (SMC)
│  └─ Register with SECP (eservices.secp.gov.pk)
│     └─ Also get NTN from FBR
│     └─ Cost: PKR 15-25K
│
├─ I have 2+ people, want a company (Pvt Ltd)
│  └─ Register with SECP (eservices.secp.gov.pk)
│     └─ Also get NTN from FBR
│     └─ Cost: PKR 20-50K
│
├─ I have 2-20 partners in a partnership
│  └─ Register with provincial Registrar of Firms
│     └─ Also get NTN from FBR
│     └─ Cost: PKR 5-10K
│
├─ I'm a freelancer earning money
│  └─ Must get NTN from FBR (iris.fbr.gov.pk)
│     └─ Cost: PKR 0 (free)
│     └─ NO SECP registration needed
│
└─ I'm a business with sales >10M
   └─ Get NTN from FBR (mandatory)
      └─ Get STRN from FBR (if selling goods/services above threshold)
      └─ Cost: Free (included in NTN/STRN)
```

## QUICK REFERENCE: Who Does What

### When You Want to Register a COMPANY
→ Go to **SECP** (not FBR for this)

### When You Want TAX STATUS
→ Go to **FBR** (everyone needs this)

### When You Want PARTNERSHIP RECOGNITION
→ Go to **Provincial Registrar of Firms**

### When You Want a BANK ACCOUNT
→ Go to your **Bank** (they'll ask for NTN from FBR)

### When You Want to APPEAL A LAW
→ Depends on what law:
- Company law issue? → SECP
- Tax issue? → FBR appeals office
- Partnership dispute? → Registrar or court

## COMMON CONFUSIONS CLEARED

### "Do I need SECP if I have NTN?"
- **If sole prop:** No. Just NTN is enough.
- **If SMC/Pvt Ltd:** Yes. Both NTN (FBR) AND SECP registration.

### "Can SECP and FBR come to audit me?"
- **FBR:** Can audit your income/taxes (always possible)
- **SECP:** Can audit your company governance (if registered with them)

### "Is a provincial Registrar the same as SECP?"
- **No.** Registrar of Firms handles partnerships (provincial).
- **SECP** handles companies (national).

### "Where do I pay taxes?"
- **FBR** (via IRIS portal online or bank)
- Not to SECP

### "What if I register with the wrong authority?"
- Common mistake by founders
- Solution: Contact correct authority, re-register
- No penalty if unintentional and corrected quickly
