# Quick Reference: Business Structures

Print this page or bookmark it. One-page summary for each structure.

---

## SOLE PROPRIETORSHIP - The Zero-Cost Option

**Best For:** Freelancers, small service providers, testing ideas

**Registration:** Only NTN (free, 1-5 days at [iris.fbr.gov.pk](https://iris.fbr.gov.pk))

**Cost:** PKR 0

**Key Facts:**
- You ARE the business (no separation)
- Personal assets at risk (unlimited liability)
- Simple tax filing
- Can upgrade to SMC later

**Status Check:**
- How many people? **1**
- Registered where? **None (just NTN)**
- Liability protection? **None**
- Need annual accounts? **Tax return only**

**When to Upgrade:** When business grows, gets risky, needs investor credibility

---

## SMC (Single Member Company) - The Sweet Spot for Solo Founders

**Best For:** Solo entrepreneurs, freelancers with assets, professionals

**Registration:** SECP (2-3 days, or 5-7 with consultant)

**Cost:** PKR 15,000-25,000

**Key Facts:**
- You're sole shareholder and director
- Separate legal entity (assets protected)
- Limited liability protection
- Required: SECP filing annually
- Can take on partner later → becomes Pvt Ltd

**Status Check:**
- How many people? **1 (shareholder)**
- Registered where? **SECP**
- Liability protection? **Limited (YES)**
- Need annual accounts? **Yes + tax return**

**Document Checklist:**
- [ ] CNIC copy
- [ ] Office address proof
- [ ] Memorandum of Association (template on SECP)
- [ ] Articles of Association
- [ ] Digital signature

**Timeline:**
- Name search: 1 hour
- Doc preparation: 2-3 days
- SECP processing: 2-3 days
- Total with consultant: 1-2 weeks

## PRIVATE LIMITED COMPANY - The Investment-Ready Structure

**Best For:** Startups seeking funding, multiple founders, growth-oriented companies

**Registration:** SECP (2-3 days, or 1-2 weeks with consultant)

**Cost:** PKR 20,000-50,000+

**Key Facts:**
- 2+ shareholders, 2+ directors required
- Separate legal entity
- Limited liability for all members
- Can issue shares to investors
- More complex compliance
- Better for institutional investors

**Status Check:**
- How many people? **2 or more**
- Registered where? **SECP**
- Liability protection? **Limited (YES)**
- Need annual accounts? **Yes + audited if large**

**Document Checklist:**
- [ ] CNICs of all founders
- [ ] Office address proof
- [ ] Memorandum of Association
- [ ] Articles of Association
- [ ] Digital signatures for all directors
- [ ] Cap table (who owns what %?)

**Timeline:**
- Name search: 1 hour
- Doc prep: 3-5 days
- SECP processing: 2-3 days
- Total with consultant: 1-2 weeks

**Annual Requirements:**
- Shareholder meeting (once a year minimum)
- Board meeting minutes
- Audited accounts (if large)
- SECP return filing
- Tax return filing

---

## PARTNERSHIP - The Multi-Owner Structure

**Best For:** 2-20 people starting business together

**Registration:** Provincial Registrar of Firms (not SECP)

**Cost:** ~PKR 5,000 (varies by province)

**Key Facts:**
- 2-20 partners required
- Not a separate legal entity
- Partners personally liable (unlimited)
- Simple to form (partnership deed is key)
- No corporate governance required
- Best for professional services

**Status Check:**
- How many people? **2-20**
- Registered where? **Registrar of Firms (provincial)**
- Liability protection? **None (unlimited)**
- Need annual accounts? **Tax filing only**

**Document Checklist:**
- [ ] Partnership deed (signed by all)
- [ ] CNICs of all partners
- [ ] Office address
- [ ] Profit-sharing agreement
- [ ] Partner responsibility breakdown

**Timeline:**
- Deed drafting: 2-3 days
- Registrar filing: 7-15 days
- Total: 2-3 weeks

**Caution:**
- One partner's mistake = all partners liable
- Requires high trust
- Harder to remove partner later

## COMPARISON TABLE

| Feature | Sole Prop | SMC | Pvt Ltd | Partnership |
|---------|-----------|-----|---------|-------------|
| **Minimum People** | 1 | 1 | 2 | 2 |
| **Registration Authority** | None (NTN only) | SECP | SECP | Registrar of Firms |
| **Liability** | Unlimited | Limited ✓ | Limited ✓ | Unlimited |
| **Cost** | PKR 0 | PKR 15-25K | PKR 20-50K | PKR 5K |
| **Setup Time** | 1-5 days | 1-2 weeks | 1-2 weeks | 2-3 weeks |
| **Annual Filing** | Tax only | SECP + Tax | SECP + Tax | Tax only |
| **Investor-Ready** | No | Yes | Yes ✓ | No |
| **Best for Startups?** | Testing | Solo formal | Funding ✓ | Group ventures |

## DECISION FLOWCHART

**Q: How many founders?**
- 1 person → Go to next Q
- 2+ people → Partnership or Pvt Ltd

**Q (if 1 person): Do you have assets to protect?**
- No, just testing → **Sole Proprietorship**
- Yes, some risk → **SMC**

**Q (if 2+ people): Planning to raise funding?**
- No, just splitting → **Partnership**
- Yes, seeking investors → **Pvt Ltd**

## FASTEST PATH: Testing an Idea
1. Get NTN (free, [iris.fbr.gov.pk](https://iris.fbr.gov.pk))
2. Open business bank account
3. Start working
4. File annual return by Sept 30
**Time: 1 week, Cost: PKR 0**

## FASTEST PATH: Solo Professional (Safe)
1. Register SMC with SECP (choose consultant for docs)
2. Get certificate in 1 week
3. Open company bank account
4. File annual returns
**Time: 1-2 weeks, Cost: PKR 15-25K**

## FASTEST PATH: Seeking Investment
1. Register Pvt Ltd with SECP
2. Get certificate within 1 week
3. Draft sharehold structure
4. Onboard investors (shares/valuation)
**Time: 1-2 weeks, Cost: PKR 20-50K**
