# Business Structure Comparison for Pakistani Founders

Choose your business structure based on your specific needs. This comprehensive comparison covers cost, liability, compliance, and growth considerations.

---

|---|---|---|---|---|

| **Liability** | ❌ Unlimited | ❌ Unlimited (all partners) | ✅ Limited | ✅ Limited |

## DETAILED COMPARISON

### 1. LEGAL STRUCTURE & SEPARATION

| Aspect | Sole Proprietorship | Partnership | SMC | Private Limited |
|--------|---|---|---|---|
| **Entity Status** | You and business are same | Partners are owners, not separate entity | Company is separate from you | Company is separate from shareholders |
| **Personal Assets Exposed** | ✅ YES - completely exposed | ✅ YES - all partners liable | ❌ NO - protected | ❌ NO - protected |
| **Property Ownership** | YOUR name (personal property) | Usually PARTNERSHIP name or partner name | COMPANY name (separate) | COMPANY name (separate) |
| **Bank Account** | Your personal account | Partnership account | Company account (separate) | Company account (separate) |
| **Contracts** | YOU sign contracts | Partners sign contracts | COMPANY signs (separate entity) | COMPANY signs (separate entity) |

**Founder Implication:** Sole prop is simplest but risky. SMC/Pvt Ltd protect your house and savings.

---

### 2. LIABILITY

| Aspect | Sole Proprietorship | Partnership | SMC | Private Limited |
|--------|---|---|---|---|
| **If Business Owes Money** | Creditors can take your personal property, bank account, house | ALL partners' personal assets exposed (even if one partner caused debt) | Company owes, not you (assets safe) | Company owes, not you (assets safe) |
| **If Sued** | You are sued personally | All partners can be sued | Company sued, not you | Company sued, not shareholders |
| **Business Failure Scenario** | Lose personal house, savings, everything | All partners lose personal house/savings | Company fails, your personal assets safe | Company fails, shareholders safe |
| **Guarantee Requirement** | Often required by banks | Often required | Sometimes required | Sometimes required |
| **Risk Level** | 🔴 HIGHEST | 🔴 HIGHEST | 🟢 LOW | 🟢 LOW |

**Real Example:**
- You run an SMC web design business
- Client sues for PKR 5 million (bad work)
- SMC loses case, owes PKR 5M
- YOUR house is SAFE (company pays from company assets only)

### 3. REGISTRATION & SETUP

| Aspect | Sole Proprietorship | Partnership | SMC | Private Limited |
|--------|---|---|---|---|
| **Where to Register** | Not with SECP; NTN only at FBR | Provincial Registrar of Firms | SECP | SECP |
| **Required Documents** | CNIC | Partnership deed, CNICs, office address, newspaper notice | CNIC, address proof, MoA, AoA, digital signature | CNICs (all), cap table, MoA, AoA, office proof, digital signatures (all) |
| **Paperwork Complexity** | 1 simple form (Form 181) | Partnership deed (legal) + newspaper | MoA/AoA (templates available) | More complex MoA/AoA + governance |
| **Registration Fee** | PKR 0 (free) | PKR 5K (varies by province) | PKR 200 (search) + PKR 1.5-20K (incorporation) | PKR 200 (search) + PKR 1.5-20K+ (incorporation varies by capital) |
| **Professional Fees** | PKR 0 (DIY) | PKR 3-8K (if using lawyer) | PKR 8-15K (consultant optional) | PKR 15-25K (consultant recommended) |
| **Total Setup Cost** | PKR 0 | PKR 5-13K | PKR 15-35K | PKR 20-75K |
| **Time to Registration** | **1-5 days** | **2-3 weeks** | **1-2 weeks** (best case) or **2-3 weeks** (with consultant) | **1-2 weeks** (best case) or **2-3 weeks** (with consultant) |

**Fastest Path:** Sole proprietorship (5 days)
**Safest Solo Path:** SMC (1-2 weeks)
**Multi-Founder Path:** Pvt Ltd (1-2 weeks)

### 8. BEST USE CASES

| Structure | Ideal For | Red Flags (Don't Use If:) | Typical Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sole Proprietorship** | Freelancers, consultants, testing ideas, low-risk services, small shops | Multiple assets to protect, anticipate hiring, plan to raise funding, large liabilities | Start in 1 week |
| **Partnership** | Professional services (lawyers, doctors, accountants), co-founders who deeply trust each other, traditional businesses | Partners don't trust each other, different goals, unclear profit split, plan to raise funding | Start in 3 weeks |
| **SMC** | Solo founders with assets, solopreneurs, professionals (architects, engineers), cautious founders, plan to grow | Immediately planning major fundraising, more than 1 shareholder, very large company vision | Start in 1-2 weeks |

## MYTHS & MISTAKES

### ❌ MYTH: "I must register with SECP to start a business"
**Truth:** Sole proprietors only need NTN (from FBR), not SECP. SECP is only for companies.

### ❌ MYTH: "SMC is just a small company"
**Truth:** SMC = Single Member Company (1 shareholder only). Cost and benefits almost identical to Pvt Ltd, but simpler governance.

### ❌ MYTH: "Partnerships are cheaper than companies"
**Truth:** Partnership registration (PKR 5K) is cheaper than SMC (PKR 15-25K), but partnership offers NO liability protection.

### ❌ MYTH: "Companies are always better"
**Truth:** For solo, freelance, low-risk work: sole proprietorship is simpler and cheaper. Company overhead only worth it if assets need protection or growth planned.

### ✅ REALITY: "Structure choice is reversible"
**Truth:** You can change from sole prop → SMC → Pvt Ltd as you grow. Plan for where you're going, but start where you are.
