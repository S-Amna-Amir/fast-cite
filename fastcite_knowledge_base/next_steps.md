# Action Plans: What to Do After Choosing Your Structure

Choose your structure below and follow the step-by-step action plan.

---

## SOLE PROPRIETORSHIP: 1-Week Launch Plan

**Timeline:** 1 week | **Cost:** PKR 0 | **Effort:** Low

### Day 1: Get NTN
- [ ] Go to [iris.fbr.gov.pk](https://iris.fbr.gov.pk)
- [ ] Click "Registration for Unregistered Person"
- [ ] Enter CNIC, name, mobile, email
- [ ] Verify OTPs
- [ ] Submit Form 181
- [ ] Note your registration number

### Day 2-3: Open Business Bank Account
- [ ] Visit bank (preferred: one near your office)
- [ ] Bring:
  - [ ] NTN certificate (download from IRIS)
  - [ ] CNIC (original)
  - [ ] Address proof (utility bill)
  - [ ] Passport (if foreigner)
- [ ] Complete account opening form
- [ ] Account activated in 1-3 days

### Day 4-5: Set Up Business Records
- [ ] Open spreadsheet or accounting app (Wave, Tally)
- [ ] Create invoice template
- [ ] Set up folder for receipts/invoices
- [ ] Organize personal and business expenses separately

### Day 6-7: Start Operations
- [ ] Announce business (website, social media, word-of-mouth)
- [ ] Send first invoice
- [ ] Start tracking income and expenses

### Mark Your Calendar
- **September 30 (Annual):** File income tax return before this date
- **August 1 (Annual):** Start collecting documents 60 days before deadline

## SMC: 2-Week Professional Registration Plan

**Timeline:** 2 weeks | **Cost:** PKR 15-25K | **Effort:** Medium

### Week 1: Preparation

**Days 1-2: Decide & Gather**
- [ ] Confirm you want SMC (read [quick_reference.md](quick_reference.md))
- [ ] Decide company name (3 options as backup)
- [ ] Identify registered office address
- [ ] Get utility bill of office (in your name)

**Days 3-4: Contact Professional**
- [ ] Email SECP consultant or lawyer
- [ ] Share company name and office address
- [ ] Request MoA/AoA template
- [ ] Agree on fee (typically PKR 5-15K)

**Days 5-6: Get Digital Signature**
- [ ] Visit digital certificate provider (Pakpost, etc.)
- [ ] Bring CNIC + passport photos
- [ ] Pay PKR 2,500-5,000
- [ ] Receive certificate (same day or next day)

**Day 7: Document Preparation**
- [ ] Review MoA/AoA templates from consultant
- [ ] Customize with company-specific details
- [ ] Print all documents

### Week 2: Filing & Registration

**Days 8-9: SECP Name Search & Filing**
- [ ] Go to [eservices.secp.gov.pk](https://eservices.secp.gov.pk)
- [ ] Check name availability
- [ ] Cost: PKR 200 (pay online)
- [ ] Note search reference number

**Days 10-11: Final Submission**
- [ ] Consultant submits Form 1 + documents to SECP
- [ ] You sign digital forms via email link
- [ ] Pay incorporation fee (PKR 1,500-3,000)
- [ ] SECP receives application

**Days 12-14: Approval & Certificate**
- [ ] SECP processes (typically 2-3 days)
- [ ] Watch email for approval notification
- [ ] Download Certificate of Incorporation
- [ ] Save 10+ copies (print + PDF)

### After Registration (Day 15+)

**Immediate:**
- [ ] Open company bank account (bring certificate + CNIC + utility bill)
- [ ] Set up accounting system
- [ ] Create company invoices/letterhead

**Within 30 days:**
- [ ] Register for NTN (if different from personal)
- [ ] Update company address with tax authorities

**Mark Your Calendar:**
- **Sept 30 (Annual):** File tax return
- **April 30 (Annual):** File SECP annual return + accounts

## PRIVATE LIMITED: 2-Week Multi-Founder Plan

**Timeline:** 2 weeks | **Cost:** PKR 20-50K | **Effort:** High

### Week 1: Founder Agreement & Planning

**Days 1-2: Cap Table Meeting**
- [ ] Meet with all founders
- [ ] Decide equity distribution (e.g., 50/30/20%)
- [ ] Assign roles (CEO, CTO, CFO, etc.)
- [ ] Decide salary/dividend structure
- [ ] Document in writing

**Days 3-4: Company Details**
- [ ] Choose 3 company name options
- [ ] Identify registered office (must be real, not virtual)
- [ ] Get utility bill of office
- [ ] Decide business objectives in 1-2 sentences

**Days 5-6: Professional Support**
- [ ] Contact SECP-registered lawyer or consultant
- [ ] Share cap table and company details
- [ ] Request MoA/AoA draft for Pvt Ltd (more complex than SMC)
- [ ] Agree on professional fee (PKR 15-30K)

**Day 7: Digital Signatures**
- [ ] Each founder gets digital signature
- [ ] Visit digital provider with ID + photos
- [ ] Cost: PKR 2,500-5,000 per person × founders
- [ ] All ready for signing by Day 7

### Week 2: Filing & Registration

**Days 8-9: Name Search & Document Finalization**
- [ ] Go to [eservices.secp.gov.pk](https://eservices.secp.gov.pk)
- [ ] Search company name availability
- [ ] Legal consultant finalizes MoA/AoA
- [ ] Cost: PKR 200 (name search)

**Days 10-11: Form 1 Preparation & Submission**
- [ ] Consultant prepares Form 1 for Pvt Ltd
- [ ] All shareholders review and understand
- [ ] All directors digitally sign Form 1
- [ ] Pay incorporation fee (PKR 5,000-20,000 depending on capital)

**Days 12-14: SECP Processing**
- [ ] Consultant files via [eservices.secp.gov.pk](https://eservices.secp.gov.pk)
- [ ] SECP processes (typically 2-3 days)
- [ ] Watch for approval email
- [ ] Download Certificate of Incorporation

### After Registration (Week 3+)

**Immediate:**
- [ ] Open company bank account (all directors may need to sign)
- [ ] Set up board meeting (even if just 2 people)
- [ ] Create share certificates for each shareholder
- [ ] Document shareholder register

**Within 30 days:**
- [ ] Register for company NTN (separate from personal)
- [ ] Prepare cap table and share register

**First Board Meeting (Within 30 days):**
- [ ] Document business plan
- [ ] Approve financial policies
- [ ] Record director decisions
- [ ] File minutes with company records

**Mark Your Calendar:**
- **Monthly:** Keep board meeting records
- **Sept 30 (Annual):** File income tax return
- **April 30 (Annual):** File SECP annual return + audited/unaudited accounts
- **Ongoing:** Fund any shareholder loans/capital contributions per agreement

## PARTNERSHIP: 3-Week Registration Plan

**Timeline:** 3 weeks | **Cost:** PKR 5-10K | **Effort:** Medium-High

### Week 1: Partnership Deed Preparation

**Days 1-2: Partner Meeting**
- [ ] Meet all partners
- [ ] Decide profit-sharing (e.g., 50/50, 40/30/30)
- [ ] Clarify roles and responsibilities
- [ ] Discuss capital contributions (who invests what)
- [ ] Plan exit strategy (what if one partner leaves)

**Days 3-5: Draft Partnership Deed**
- [ ] Use template (available from provincial registrar or lawyer)
- [ ] Include:
  - [ ] Partner names and CNICs
  - [ ] Partner addresses
  - [ ] Capital contributions of each partner
  - [ ] Profit-sharing ratio
  - [ ] Management decision-making authority
  - [ ] How disputes are resolved
  - [ ] Exit clause (what if partner leaves)
  - [ ] Bank signatory authority
- [ ] All partners review and agree

**Days 6-7: Finalize & Sign**
- [ ] Print 3-4 copies
- [ ] All partners sign each copy (in front of witness/notary recommended)
- [ ] Get notarized (provides legal backup)
- [ ] Cost: PKR 500-1,000

### Week 2: Newspaper Notice & Registration

**Days 8-9: Publish Newspaper Notice**
- [ ] Visit local newspaper office
- [ ] Publish partnership notice (required by law)
- [ ] Notice format: Partnership name + partners + office address
- [ ] Cost: PKR 1,000-3,000
- [ ] Get published copy for records

**Days 10-15: Registrar Application**
- [ ] Locate your provincial Registrar of Firms office
  - [ ] Punjab: Lahore/Rawalpindi
  - [ ] Sindh: Karachi
  - [ ] KPK: Peshawar
  - [ ] Balochistan: Quetta
- [ ] Prepare application package:
  - [ ] Original signed partnership deed
  - [ ] CNICs of all partners
  - [ ] Office address proof (utility bill)
  - [ ] Published newspaper notice
  - [ ] Registration fee (varies, typically PKR 2,000-5,000)
- [ ] Submit to registrar

### Week 3: Approval & Banking

**Days 16-21: Registrar Processing**
- [ ] Registrar processes application (7-15 days typical)
- [ ] May have minor queries (respond quickly)
- [ ] Receive partnership registration certificate
- [ ] Cost: Already paid

**After Registration:**
- [ ] Open partnership bank account
  - [ ] Bring: Partnership certificate + all partners' CNICs + office proof
  - [ ] Register all signatories
  - [ ] Set spending limits per partner
- [ ] Get NTN (separate for partnership)
- [ ] Set up accounting system

**Mark Your Calendar:**
- **Sept 30 (Annual):** Each partner files individual tax return showing their share
- **April 30 (Annual):** Prepare partnership accounts
- **Quarterly:** Review profit sharing with all partners

---

## GENERAL "ALWAYS DO" CHECKLIST

Regardless of structure:

- [ ] **Mark Sept 30** on every calendar (tax return deadline)
- [ ] **Separate accounts** (business ≠ personal money)
- [ ] **Keep receipts** (5-year record retention)
- [ ] **File on time** (missing Sept 30 = catastrophic)
- [ ] **Bank transfers** (create audit trail, avoid cash)
- [ ] **Annual review** (look at bank statements, reconcile)
