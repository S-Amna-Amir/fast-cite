# Freelancer Tax Guide for Pakistani Founders

## Summary
Pakistan offers favorable tax treatment for IT/ITeS export services. Foreign-earned income may be taxed at final reduced rates. Local income follows standard tax slabs.

## Registration Basics
1. **Get NTN First:** Register free at [FBR IRIS](https://iris.fbr.gov.pk)
2. **Declare Occupation:** Select "IT Professional" or relevant category
3. **No Separate Registration:** NTN covers both domestic and export income
4. **Bank Account:** Open business account with your NTN

## Filing Expectations
- **Annual Return:** File by September 30 every year
- **Form:** Generally Form 1 (Individual Income)
- **Documentation:** Keep invoices, receipts, and payment proofs
- **Income Disclosure:** Declare all income (foreign and local)

## Tax Rates on Export Income

| Annual Export Income | Final Tax Rate |
|---|---|
| Up to PKR 5 million | 0.25% |
| PKR 5-10 million | 0.5% |
| Above PKR 10 million | 1% |

## Local Income Tax
- Taxed under normal income tax slabs
- Deductions available for business expenses
- Progressive rates (5% at lower end, up to 35% at highest)

## Practical Tips
- Keep separate records for export and local income
- Invoice all clients (domestic or foreign)
- Maintain proof of payment (bank transfers preferred)
- File returns on time (penalties after September 30)
- Consider tax professional for audit-ready records
- Export certifications available from customs for cross-checking

## Key Benefits
- Significantly lower rates on foreign income
- Simple compliance for small export amounts
- No advance tax if filing on time

Source: [FBR Income Tax Information](https://fbr.gov.pk) | [Section 153(2) IT Export Services](https://fbr.gov.pk)
