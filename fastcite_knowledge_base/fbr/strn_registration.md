# Sales Tax Registration (STRN) for Businesses

## Summary
STRN is separate from NTN and required to legally collect and remit sales tax. If your business meets the threshold, STRN is mandatory.

## Applicability
You need STRN if:
- Annual turnover exceeds PKR 10 million (retailers/wholesalers)
- You are a manufacturer at any turnover
- You are an importer at any turnover
- You are registered as a sales tax group

## Required Information
- NTN number
- Business name and address
- Nature of business
- Annual turnover estimate
- Bank account details
- Authorized signatory details

## Application Steps
1. Log in to [FBR IRIS Portal](https://iris.fbr.gov.pk) with your NTN
2. Navigate to Sales Tax Registration
3. Select "New STRN Registration"
4. Fill business location, type, and turnover details
5. Provide bank account information
6. Submit signed declaration
7. Approval typically within 7-10 business days

## Ongoing Compliance
- **Monthly Returns:** File monthly sales tax return by the 15th of following month
- **Input Tax:** Can claim input sales tax on business purchases
- **Invoicing:** Must issue tax invoices for all taxable supplies
- **Record Keeping:** Maintain invoices and purchase records for 5 years
- **Zero-rated Supplies:** Special rules apply for exports

## Key Points
- Applicable from first business transaction if threshold is crossed
- Sales tax standard rate is 18% (some items at lower rates)
- Quarterly adjustments now common (check current rules)
- Penalties apply for late filings

Source: [FBR Sales Tax Information](https://fbr.gov.pk) | [Sales Tax Act 1990](https://fbr.gov.pk/scat/)
