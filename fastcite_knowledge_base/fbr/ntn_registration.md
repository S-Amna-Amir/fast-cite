# National Tax Number (NTN) Registration for Founders

## Summary
NTN registration is free and done online through the FBR IRIS portal. For individuals, your CNIC is used as your NTN. Required for business bank accounts and tax compliance.

## Who Needs NTN
- Business owners and entrepreneurs
- Freelancers offering services
- Sole proprietors
- Anyone earning taxable income in Pakistan

## Required Information
- Valid CNIC number
- Full name as per CNIC
- Mobile number
- Email address
- Occupation/business type

## Application Steps
1. Visit [FBR IRIS Portal](https://iris.fbr.gov.pk)
2. Click on "Registration for Unregistered Person"
3. Enter your CNIC and select "Individual"
4. Verify OTP sent to registered mobile
5. Complete Form 181 with personal details
6. Verify email OTP
7. Review and submit
8. Approval typically within 1-5 business days

## Common Pitfalls
- Using incorrect CNIC number (delays registration)
- Inactive mobile number (OTP delivery fails)
- Mismatch between CNIC name and form entry
- Waiting for physical certificate (use digital NTN from IRIS immediately)

## Key Points
- Registration is completely free
- No documents need to be uploaded
- Digital NTN is valid instantly for bank accounts
- NTN ≠ Filer status (you must file returns separately)

Source: [FBR IRIS Portal](https://iris.fbr.gov.pk)
