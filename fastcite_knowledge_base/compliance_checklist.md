# Compliance Checklist for Pakistani Founders

Use this checklist based on your business structure. Check these items every year before September 30.

---

## SOLE PROPRIETORSHIP Checklist

**Tax Compliance (FBR)**
- [ ] Calculate total income from all sources
- [ ] Collect receipts and invoices for all expenses
- [ ] Reconcile bank statements
- [ ] Calculate taxable income (revenue - deductions)
- [ ] File income tax return by **September 30**
- [ ] Keep all documents for 5 years
- [ ] If STRN registered: file monthly sales tax returns by 15th each month

**Banking & Financial**
- [ ] Maintain separate business bank account
- [ ] Record all income deposits in books
- [ ] Keep purchase invoices organized
- [ ] POS/receipt records if applicable

**Optional for Growth**
- [ ] Once turnover exceeds PKR 10M, register for STRN
- [ ] Consider upgrading to SMC for asset protection

---

## SMC (Single Member Company) Checklist

**SECP Compliance**
- [ ] File annual return to SECP (within 4 months of year-end, typically April 30)
- [ ] Prepare financial statements/accounts
- [ ] Note any changes to company details (address, business type)
- [ ] Pay SECP annual fee (~PKR 2,000-5,000)
- [ ] Maintain minute book (if held any board meetings)

**FBR (Tax) Compliance**
- [ ] Calculate company income
- [ ] Collect business expense receipts
- [ ] File income tax return by **September 30**
- [ ] If turnover > PKR 10M and sales occur: register and file STRN returns monthly

**Record Keeping**
- [ ] Maintain accounting records for 5 years
- [ ] Board resolutions if any major decisions
- [ ] Director meeting minutes
- [ ] Shareholder (you) records

**Banking & Financial**
- [ ] Company bank account statements reconciled
- [ ] All transactions recorded in books
- [ ] Payroll records if you pay yourself salary

## PRIVATE LIMITED COMPANY Checklist

**SECP Compliance (Higher Scrutiny)**
- [ ] File annual return with SECP (within 4 months of year-end)
- [ ] Prepare audited financial statements (if turnover > PKR 30M)
- [ ] Prepare unaudited accounts (if turnover ≤ PKR 30M)
- [ ] Hold annual shareholder/director meeting
- [ ] Pass resolutions for:
  - [ ] Approval of accounts
  - [ ] Dividend declaration (if applicable)
  - [ ] Director appointments (if changes)
- [ ] Pay SECP annual fee
- [ ] Update any director/shareholder changes with SECP

**FBR (Tax) Compliance**
- [ ] Audit by approved chartered accountant (if required)
- [ ] Calculate company taxable income
- [ ] File income tax return by **September 30**
- [ ] If turnover > PKR 10M and sales occur: file STRN returns monthly

**Record Keeping - Stricter**
- [ ] Accounting records for 5 years
- [ ] Meeting minutes and board resolutions
- [ ] Shareholder register (who owns what %)
- [ ] Director register
- [ ] Details of all major contracts
- [ ] Share transfer documents (if applicable)

**Banking & Financial**
- [ ] Company bank reconciliation and statements
- [ ] Cash flow tracking
- [ ] Payroll records for all employees
- [ ] All supplier invoices and payments

**Regulatory Updates**
- [ ] Check SECP website for any rule changes
- [ ] Monitor FBR tax changes via [fbr.gov.pk](https://fbr.gov.pk)

---

## PARTNERSHIP Checklist

**Registrar of Firms (Local/Provincial)**
- [ ] Verify partnership deed registration is current
- [ ] If any changes to partners: update with registrar
- [ ] Pay annual registration fee (if applicable in your province)

**FBR (Tax) Compliance**
- [ ] Calculate partnership firm's total income
- [ ] Allocate profit/loss among partners per deed ratios
- [ ] Each partner files **individual** return showing their share
- [ ] File by **September 30**
- [ ] If turnover > PKR 10M and sales occur: register and file STRN returns monthly

**Record Keeping**
- [ ] Partnership deed on file
- [ ] Accounting records for 5 years
- [ ] Profit/loss distribution documents
- [ ] Partner capital accounts
- [ ] Bank statements and reconciliation

**Banking & Financial**
- [ ] Partnership bank account statements
- [ ] All business transactions recorded
- [ ] Partner withdrawal/contribution records

**Communication**
- [ ] Annual review of profit-sharing with all partners
- [ ] Discuss any business changes
- [ ] Update partnership deed if roles change

## IMPORTANT DEADLINES (All Structures)

| Task | Deadline | Penalty if Late |
|------|----------|-----------------|
| Annual Income Tax Return | September 30 | Filer status loss, daily penalties, advance tax increase |
| SECP Annual Return (Companies) | 4 months after year-end (April 30) | Filer penalty, company status risk |
| Monthly Sales Tax Return (STRN) | 15th of next month | 10% penalty per month |
| SECP Annual Fee | Within year | Company delisting risk |

---

## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

- ❌ Missing September 30 deadline (catastrophic - lose filer status)
- ❌ Not keeping receipts/invoices (can't prove deductions)
- ❌ Mixing personal and business accounts (audit nightmare)
- ❌ Ignoring STRN threshold (legal violation if applicable)
- ❌ Late sales tax filing (compounding penalties)
- ❌ Not maintaining company records (SECP fine risk)
