# Common Mistakes Pakistani Founders Make

Learn from others' mistakes. Avoid these to protect your business.

---

## ALL STRUCTURES: Critical Mistakes

### ❌ Missing September 30 Tax Return Deadline
**What happens:** Lose filer status, daily penalties, advance tax increases from 15% to 25%+

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Set phone reminder on August 1
- [ ] Gather documents by September 15
- [ ] File by September 30 even if zero income
- [ ] File 1-2 days early for safety (e-filing can have technical issues)

**Recovery:** Can apply for reinstatement of filer status with FBR (takes 2-3 months, costs penalty)

---

### ❌ Mixing Personal and Business Money
**What happens:** Tax audit complications, can't prove business income vs personal

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Open separate business bank account immediately
- [ ] Use business account ONLY for business (no personal shopping)
- [ ] Keep personal account separate
- [ ] Ask for invoices/receipts for all business purchases
- [ ] Use debit card for business, personal card for personal

**Audit nightmare:** Auditor disallows deductions, adds penalties

---

### ❌ Not Keeping Receipts and Invoices
**What happens:** FBR doesn't accept claimed deductions (no proof = no deduction)

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Get receipt for EVERY business expense (no matter how small)
- [ ] Store digitally (photo or scan) + physical copy
- [ ] Organize by month/category
- [ ] Keep for minimum 5 years
- [ ] Use accounting app (Wave, Tally) to track

**Lost money:** Claimed PKR 500K deductions but only got PKR 200K accepted (denied 60%)

---

### ❌ Filing Correct Income But Forgetting Sources
**What happens:** FBR asks "where did this money come from?" and you have no proof

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Bank statements show all deposits
- [ ] Keep client contracts/agreements
- [ ] Invoices show who paid you
- [ ] Invoice numbers in your records
- [ ] Digital audit trail via bank transfers (better than cash)

**FBR action:** Deemed income addition or rejection of filing

## SOLE PROPRIETORSHIP: Specific Mistakes

### ❌ Not Registering for STRN When Needed
**Threshold Alert:** If annual sales cross PKR 10M (retail/wholesale) or any amount (manufacturing), STRN is mandatory

**What happens:** Unlicensed sales tax collection = violation, penalties, legal issues

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Track monthly sales religiously
- [ ] When nearing PKR 10M, apply for STRN (takes 7-10 days)
- [ ] File monthly returns by 15th after
- [ ] Can claim input tax once registered

**Penalty:** 10% of tax due + recovery action by FBR

---

### ❌ Staying Sole Proprietor With Significant Assets
**Mistake:** Growing business but keeping unlimited liability

**Why risky:**
- Business debt = your personal debt
- Creditor sues = can take personal house, car, savings
- Not just business risk; existential risk

**When to upgrade:** Once you have PKR 1M+ assets or business generates PKR 500K+ annual profit

**Action:** Register SMC (2 weeks, PKR 15-25K) = protect assets

## SMC: Specific Mistakes

### ❌ Not Filing Annual Return to SECP
**Deadline:** Within 4 months of year-end (usually April 30)

**What happens:** SECP strikes off company (delists it), company becomes invalid

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Mark April 15 as deadline (give buffer)
- [ ] Prepare financial statement by March 31
- [ ] File to SECP by April 30
- [ ] Keep confirmation email

**Recovery:** Can apply for restoration (FBR involved, takes 3-6 months, costs extra)

---

### ❌ Not Maintaining Accounting Records
**What should be kept:**
- Monthly bank reconciliation
- Invoice register
- Purchase register
- Payroll (if employees)
- Board minutes (even if just you)

**What happens:** Audit fails, penalties for record violations, rejection of deductions

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Use accounting software (Tally, Wave, Xero)
- [ ] Enter transactions as they happen (not once yearly)
- [ ] Bank reconcile every month
- [ ] Archive documents quarterly

---

### ❌ Not Paying SECP Annual Fee
**Mistake:** Filing annual return but forgetting to pay annual fee (typically PKR 2,000-5,000)

**What happens:** Company delisting, loss of good standing status

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Check SECP eServices for fee amount
- [ ] Pay with annual return filing
- [ ] Keep receipt
- [ ] Calendar reminder set

## PRIVATE LIMITED: Specific Mistakes

### ❌ Not Holding Annual Shareholder Meeting
**Requirement:** At least ONE meeting per year with shareholders (even if just 2 people)

**What happens:** Regulatory violation, SECP penalty, accounts rejected

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Schedule meeting for October (after fiscal year)
- [ ] Document meeting minutes
- [ ] Board resolution on dividend, director changes, etc.
- [ ] All shareholders sign minutes
- [ ] File copy with SECP

**Meeting should cover:**
- Approval of annual accounts
- Dividend declaration
- Director changes
- Major business decisions

---

### ❌ Not Auditing Accounts (When Required)
**Rule:** If annual turnover > PKR 30M, must have audited accounts by chartered accountant

**Mistake:** Submitting un-audited accounts to SECP = violation

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Check if you crossed PKR 30M threshold
- [ ] If yes, hire chartered accountant (PKR 50-150K cost)
- [ ] Get accounts audited by March 31 (fiscal year end)
- [ ] File audited version to SECP

**Penalty:** SECP can fine company or reject annual return

---

### ❌ Not Updating Shareholder/Director Changes
**Mistake:** Partner joins/leaves or ownership changes, but not updated with SECP

**What happens:** SECP records out of date, can't prove ownership, transfer issues later

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] When shareholder changes: File change notice to SECP
- [ ] When director changes: File change notice
- [ ] Forms available on [eservices.secp.gov.pk](https://eservices.secp.gov.pk)
- [ ] Do within 30 days of change

**Problem later:** Investor due diligence fails, can't sell shares cleanly

## PARTNERSHIP: Specific Mistakes

### ❌ No Written Partnership Deed Or Weak Deed
**Mistake:** Handshake deal, no written terms

**What happens:** Dispute over profit-sharing, no way to resolve

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Formal written deed BEFORE business starts
- [ ] Specify profit-sharing percentage
- [ ] Exit clause (what if partner wants to leave?)
- [ ] Dispute resolution process
- [ ] Capital contributions
- [ ] Management roles

**Costly mistake:** Partners split into legal battle, business collapses

---

### ❌ Not Registering Partnership With Registrar
**Mistake:** Claiming partnership but not registered

**What happens:** Not legally recognized, harder to enforce agreements, bank account issues

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Publish deed in newspaper (required)
- [ ] File with provincial Registrar of Firms
- [ ] Get partnership certificate
- [ ] Register office address

**Timeline:** Takes 2-3 weeks, costs PKR 5K

---

### ❌ One Partner's Debt Becomes Everyone's Debt
**Partnership risk:** Joint and several liability

**Example:** Partner A takes personal loan but can't pay. Creditor can sue all partners for full amount.

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Set clear rules in deed: no personal debt commitments without consent
- [ ] Partner personal debts ≠ partnership debt
- [ ] Avoid co-signing personal loans as partnership
- [ ] Keep business and personal finances separate

**Protection:** Good deed + insurance, but limited

---

### ❌ Not Tracking Profit-Sharing Legally
**Mistake:** Years go by, partners disagree on profit sharing

**What happens:** Can't prove who gets what %, argument escalates

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Partnership deed specifies exact percentage
- [ ] Annual accounting shows profit calculated correctly
- [ ] Profit distributed per deed
- [ ] Document distribution (transfer to accounts)
- [ ] All partners sign yearly profit statement

## TAX-SPECIFIC MISTAKES (All Structures)

### ❌ Claiming Black Money Income
**Huge risk:** Showing income without source documentation

**Example:** "Made PKR 5M, but only has PKR 1M in bank deposits"

**What happens:** FBR disallows income, adds penalties, interest, potential prosecution for tax evasion

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Document ALL income sources
- [ ] Use bank transfers (leave audit trail)
- [ ] Cash income? Keep invoices + client records
- [ ] Be conservative; claim only documented income

---

### ❌ Excessive Deductions Without Proof
**Mistake:** Claiming PKR 2M deductions but only PKR 500K in receipts

**What happens:** Auditor rejects deductions, adds penalties

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] Only claim documented expenses
- [ ] Deductions reasonable (don't claim luxury as office)
- [ ] Get receipt for every expense
- [ ] Percentage-wise: actual deductions ÷ income = reasonable ratio

**Audit trigger:** Deductions > 60-70% of income = red flag

---

### ❌ Not Filing on Time Even If No Income
**Mistake:** "I made no money so why file?"

**What happens:** Lose filer status, future problems with loans/investment

**How to prevent:**
- [ ] File even with zero income
- [ ] Shows regularity and compliance
- [ ] Maintains filer status
- [ ] Prevents future assumptions of hiding income
