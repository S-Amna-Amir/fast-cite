# Employee Tax & Payroll Management for Pakistani Businesses

When you hire your first employee, new tax obligations begin. This guide explains what employers must do.

---

## OVERVIEW: Employer Tax Obligations

When you pay an employee salary, you become responsible for:
1. **Withholding Tax** (tax on employee salary)
2. **EOBI Contributions** (retirement fund)
3. **Employee Record Keeping** (5 years)
4. **Compliance Filings** (quarterly + annual)

---

## SALARY TAX BASICS

### What is Withholding Tax?

**Definition:** Tax deducted from employee salary before they receive it.

**Example:**
- Employee monthly salary: PKR 100,000
- Withholding tax: PKR 10,000 (depends on slab)
- Employee receives: PKR 90,000
- You remit to FBR: PKR 10,000

### Who Pays Withholding Tax?
- ✅ Employers (you deduct from salary)
- ❌ NOT employees (you handle it)

### Tax Rates (FY 2025-26) - Subject to Change

| Monthly Salary | Tax Rate |
|---|---|
| Up to PKR 50,000 | 0% (exempt) |
| PKR 50,000 - 100,000 | 5% |
| PKR 100,000 - 200,000 | 10% |
| PKR 200,000 - 500,000 | 15% |
| Above PKR 500,000 | 20% |

**Important:** Rates change annually. Verify with FBR before deducting.

## EOBI - Employees' Old-Age Benefits

### What is EOBI?
**Definition:** Retirement/pension fund contribution for employees.

### Who Contributes?
- **Employer:** 5% of salary
- **Employee:** 3% of salary (deducted from salary)
- **Total:** 8% combined

### Calculation Example
```
Monthly salary: PKR 100,000

Employer contribution (5%): PKR 5,000 (you pay)
Employee contribution (3%): PKR 3,000 (deduct from their salary)
Total EOBI: PKR 8,000
```

### When Contributions Start
- Usually starts after 90 days of employment
- Both employer and employee must have registered NTN

### EOBI Remittance
- Quarterly to EOBI office
- Employer contributes + employee contribution together
- Due within 30 days of quarter end

## OTHER PAYROLL DEDUCTIONS

### Workmen Compensation Insurance
- **Requirement:** If you have 5+ employees
- **Payment:** Employer pays to WBIS (Workers Board Insurance Scheme)
- **Rate:** 0.3-1% of salary (varies by industry risk)
- **Frequency:** Annual or quarterly

### Health Insurance
- **Requirement:** If you have 10+ employees (varies by province)
- **Payment:** Employer may pay or employee contribution
- **Frequency:** Monthly or quarterly

### Employee Income Tax (Separate from Withholding)
- After all deductions, annual income tax is calculated
- Can be offsetted against withholding tax paid
- Reconciled in annual return

## MONTHLY PAYROLL PROCESS

### Step 1: Calculate Salary
```
Basic salary: PKR 100,000

Additions:
+ Allowances: PKR 20,000
+ Bonus: PKR 0
= Gross: PKR 120,000

Deductions:
- Withholding tax (10%): PKR 12,000
- EOBI (employee 3%): PKR 3,600
- Other deductions: PKR 0
= Net salary paid: PKR 104,400

Employer pays additionally:
+ EOBI employer (5%): PKR 6,000
+ Workmen comp: PKR 300
= Total employer cost: PKR 110,700
```

### Step 2: Pay Employee
- [ ] Transfer PKR 104,400 to employee bank account
- [ ] Keep record of payment slip

### Step 3: Document
- [ ] Print/save payment slip for records
- [ ] Maintain attendance records
- [ ] Note any leaves/absences

### Step 4: Maintain Register
Keep employee register with:
- Name, CNIC, salary
- Tax withheld each month
- EOBI contributions
- Leave balance

## QUARTERLY FILINGS

### FBR Withholding Tax Submission
- **Frequency:** Quarterly (within 30 days of quarter end)
- **How:** Via [fbr.gov.pk](https://fbr.gov.pk) online portal
- **What:** Report total salary paid + tax withheld to all employees
- **Cost:** Free

### EOBI Contribution Submission
- **Frequency:** Quarterly (within 30 days of quarter end)
- **How:** Via EOBI office (in-person or online)
- **What:** Employer + employee EOBI contributions
- **Cost:** Contribution amount + small admin fee

### Workmen Compensation
- **Frequency:** Quarterly or annual (depends on policy)
- **How:** To WBIS office
- **What:** Premium payment for all employees

---

## ANNUAL REQUIREMENTS

### Year-End Tax Return
- **Filing:** With annual business tax return (by Sept 30)
- **Contents:** Summary of all employees + total salary + tax deducted
- **Record:** Keep attendance/salary registers for 5 years

### Employee Contracts
- **Required:** Yes (mandatory for all employees)
- **Content:** Salary, benefits, duties, resignation clause
- **Record:** Keep in file for contract duration + 2 years

### Compliance Verification
- [ ] All employees have been withheld tax calculated correctly
- [ ] All quarterly filings completed
- [ ] EOBI contributions up to date
- [ ] Workmen compensation paid
- [ ] Leave records maintained
- [ ] Records saved for 5 years

## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### ❌ Mistake 1: Not Withholding Tax
**Problem:** Employee owes back taxes + penalties

**Fix:**
- Start withholding immediately
- Catch up amounts in next filing
- File amended return explaining

---

### ❌ Mistake 2: Wrong Tax Calculation
**Problem:** Withheld too much or too little

**Fix:**
- Verify rates with FBR annually
- Adjust prospectively
- File reconciliation in annual return

---

### ❌ Mistake 3: Delaying EOBI Payment
**Problem:** EOBI interest + penalties accumulate

**Fix:**
- Pay EOBI within 30 days of quarter end
- Keep proof of payment
- Catch up if late

---

### ❌ Mistake 4: No Employment Contract
**Problem:** Disputes + no legal protection

**Fix:**
- Create simple written contract
- Sign with each employee
- Keep for record

---

### ❌ Mistake 5: Not Keeping Payroll Records
**Problem:** FBR audit = can't prove payments made

**Fix:**
- Maintain salary register
- Keep bank transfer receipts
- Attach to tax return

---

## CASH VS BANK TRANSFERS

### Cash Salary (Not Recommended)
- ❌ Hard to prove payment (no trail)
- ❌ Tax audit risk
- ❌ Employee can deny receiving
- ❌ EOBI hard to substantiate

### Bank Transfer (Recommended)
- ✅ Clear audit trail
- ✅ FBR can verify instantly
- ✅ Receipt proof automatically
- ✅ Professional image

**Rule of thumb:** Use bank transfers for all employee payments.

## EMPLOYEE RIGHTS

### Minimum Wage
- Pakistan sets **minimum wage** (changes yearly)
- **Current (2026):** Approximately PKR 32,000-35,000/month
- **Your obligation:** Pay at least minimum wage

### Provident Fund (Optional)
- Some employers offer optional 401k-style fund
- Employee + employer contribute
- Paid on retirement

### Overtime/Extra Hours
- Overtime usually 1.5x to 2x rate
- Regulate with labor department rules
- Document carefully

### Leave (Vacation/Sick)
- Employees entitled to annual leave
- Accrue: ~2-2.5 days per month
- Some companies pay lump sum on separation

## WHEN TO HIRE A PROFESSIONAL

### Hire an Accountant If:
- [ ] You have 5+ employees (compliance gets complex)
- [ ] You're unsure about tax rates
- [ ] You've missed any quarterly filings
- [ ] Workmen compensation is involved

### Accountant Cost
- **Small business:** PKR 5,000-15,000/month
- **Large business:** PKR 20,000-50,000/month

### What They Handle
- Salary processing
- Tax withholding calculations
- EOBI filings
- Quarterly/annual compliance
- Record keeping
