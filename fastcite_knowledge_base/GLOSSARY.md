# FastCite Legal & Tax Glossary

## Business Structure Terms

**Sole Proprietorship**
You and your business are the same legal entity. No company registration. Unlimited personal liability.

**Private Limited Company (Pvt Ltd)**
A separate legal entity registered with SECP. Shareholders have limited liability. Requires minimum 2 shareholders and 2 directors.

**Single Member Company (SMC)**
A private company with only 1 shareholder. Still a separate entity with limited liability. Cheaper than Pvt Ltd, better than sole proprietorship.

**Partnership**
2-20 people in a business relationship. Registered with provincial Registrar of Firms (not SECP). Partners have unlimited liability.

**Limited Liability**
Personal assets are protected from business debts. If company owes money, creditors cannot take your personal property.

**Unlimited Liability**
Personal assets are exposed. If business fails or gets sued, creditors can take personal property, bank accounts, etc.

## Tax Terms

**NTN (National Tax Number)**
Your tax ID issued by FBR. Required for business bank accounts and tax filing. Given as a number linked to your CNIC.

**Filer Status**
Official FBR designation after you file your first tax return on time. Carries benefits like lower advance tax. Loss of filer status = penalties.

**STRN (Sales Tax Registration Number)**
Tax ID for businesses that collect sales tax. Required if turnover exceeds PKR 10M (or any amount if manufacturer/importer).

**Final Tax**
A fixed tax rate on income instead of normal tax slabs. Freelancers on export income pay final tax (0.25-1%). No other deductions allowed.

**Advance Tax**
Tax paid upfront (before year-end) based on estimated income. Filers may avoid if filing on time with proper income estimate.

**Tax Slab**
Progressive tax rate. Low income = lower %. High income = higher %. Pakistan: ranges from 5% to 35%.

**Input Tax**
Sales tax paid on business purchases. STRN-registered businesses can claim this against collected sales tax.

**Deduction**
Business expense you subtract from income before calculating tax. Examples: rent, software, subscriptions.

## Registration & Compliance Terms

**SECP (Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan)**
Government body that registers and oversees companies (Pvt Ltd, SMC, etc.).

**Certificate of Incorporation**
Official document proving your company is registered with SECP. Required for bank accounts, contracts, etc.

**Memorandum of Association (MoA)**
Document showing company's objectives and general rules. Core incorporation document.

**Articles of Association (AoA)**
Document detailing internal management and governance of company. How directors are chosen, meetings held, etc.

**Digital Signature**
Electronic signature legally valid for SECP filings. Required for company registration forms.

**Form 1**
SECP's incorporation form. Filed to register a new company (private limited or SMC).

**Annual Return**
Yearly filing by companies to SECP and individuals to FBR. Shows income, expenses, ownership structure.

**Registrar of Firms**
Provincial authority (not SECP) that registers partnership firms. Each province has its own office.

**Partnership Deed**
Written agreement between partners explaining profit-sharing, responsibilities, dispute resolution. Essential legal document.

## Liability & Legal Terms

**Creditor**
Person or entity the business owes money to. Can be lender, supplier, etc.

**Judgment/Judgment Debt**
Court decision ordering you to pay money. If unpaid, creditor can attach assets.

**Joint and Several Liability**
In partnership: each partner liable for full debt, not just their share. One partner's mistake = all partners at risk.

**Asset Protection**
Legal separation between business and personal assets. Limited liability structures provide this.

**Liquidation**
Closing a business: selling assets, paying debts, distributing remaining money to owners.

---

## Financial Terms

**Turnover**
Total revenue (before expenses). Used to determine tax registration requirements.

**Profit/Loss**
Revenue minus expenses. If positive = profit; if negative = loss.

**Capital**
Money invested to start the business. In companies, shown as share capital.

**Shareholder**
Person who owns part of a company (owns "shares").

**Director**
Person managing/running the company on behalf of shareholders.

**Dividend**
Profit distribution to shareholders. Must be approved by board.

## Useful Comparisons

**Company vs Sole Proprietorship**
- Company: Separate entity, limited liability, more compliance
- Sole Prop: Simple to start, unlimited liability, minimal paperwork

**NTN vs STRN**
- NTN: Income tax registration (all business owners)
- STRN: Sales tax registration (only if selling goods/services above threshold)

**Filer vs Non-Filer**
- Filer: Filed timely returns, lower advance tax, better credibility
- Non-Filer: Haven't filed or filed late, higher tax rates, limited credit
